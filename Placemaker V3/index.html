About

A journey that began almost four decades ago, comes to fruition.

Our founder, Gregory Zurlo, spent his early years working through the building trades where he found his first passion for architecture. Over the years, he's worked for firms in the Greater Boston Area fulfilling responsibilites as Director of Project Management, Architect, and Construction Admistrator.

With his ambitions set on practicing professional real estate development on his own, he, with the help of his son, Avi Zurlo, and a team of close investors and advisors, has created Placemaker.
